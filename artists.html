UK-based independent record label championing innovative music.

Cavallini/Nada

Giulia Cavallini works at the intersection of sound, the body, and presence. As a practitioner of sound healing, she brings gongs and singing bowls as vessels for vibration. She is a member of the Planetary Gong Ensemble.

Karlos Nada is an electronic producer with a restless curiosity and a highly developed ear for space and texture. His work draws on the fringes of ambient music and electronic composition.

Capitol K

Capitol K is the alias of Kristian Craig Robinson, musician and producer known for his eclectic and exploratory approach to sound. Since the late 1990s, Capitol K has continually reinvented his music, moving fluidly across various sonic landscapes. A recurring theme throughout his work is psycho-geographic audio wandering.

Listen on Bandcamp

John Johanna

John Johanna is the nom de guerre of Ben MacDiarmid, a singular English singer-songwriter whose varied output has run from initial cassette forays in minimal rock and psych-folk to 2022’s Ahimsa EP, a futurist soundscape made with Mandé jali Sefo Kanuteh. In 2024, Johanna’s talents extended to the silver screen, composing the score for Day of the Fight, a feature film that premiered at the Venice Film Festival.

Mermaid Chunky

The Romance of Baba Loco

Originally a bedroom recording project by multi-instrumentalist Samuel Huxley, ROBL evolved into a collective of multi-disciplinary artists from various countries. Their music is characterized by deep, evocative melodies and ambient soundscapes.

Listen on Bandcamp

Sami El-Enany & Dampé

Sami El-Enany is a British Egyptian composer and producer whose work negotiates the fringes of modern classical, electronica and field recording. Dampé is a studio project from South East London deeply involved in club culture.

Sebastian Reynolds

Sebastian Reynolds is a music producer and composer based between Oxford and Swindon, UK. Working in score for contemporary dance and film.
