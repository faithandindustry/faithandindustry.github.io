Cavallini/Nada — Continuum

FAI037 • Ambient / Electronic / Experimental

Album Release: 5th June 2025

  4. Continuum Pt 4

Continuum is the debut album from sound healer Giulia Cavallini and electronic producer Karlos Nada. It is the sound of two friends who have spent years listening deeply — both to music and to one another — finally deciding to make something together.

The album features the flute of Jin Theriault and creates a spacious, unhurried sonic world where gongs, singing bowls, and subtle electronics breathe together.
