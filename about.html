About Faith and Industry

Faith and Industry is a London-based independent record label founded and run by musician and producer Capitol K (Kristian Craig Robinson).

Since the late 1990s, the label has been a home for innovative, boundary-pushing music. With a strong emphasis on quality short-run vinyl pressings and carefully curated digital releases, Faith and Industry champions artists who explore new sonic territories — from psych-folk and acoustic songwriting to experimental electronics, psychedelic dub, and beyond.

The roster includes:

  • John Johanna
  • Capitol K
  • Ursa Major Moving Group
  • James Howard
  • Mermaid Chunky
  • The Romance of Baba Loco
  • Sami El-Enany & Dampé
  • Sebastian Reynolds
  • Champagne Dub

Releases are distributed internationally via SRD / IDOL and are regularly praised by BBC Radio 6 Music presenters including Don Letts, Lauren Laverne, and Tom Robinson.

Faith and Industry remains proudly independent, focusing on artistic integrity, beautiful physical releases, and supporting the long-term careers of its artists.
