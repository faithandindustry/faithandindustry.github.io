About Faith and Industry

Faith and Industry is a London-based independent record label founded and run by musician and producer Capitol K (Kristian Craig Robinson).

Since the late 1990s, the label has been a home for innovative, boundary-pushing music. With a strong emphasis on quality short-run vinyl pressings and carefully curated digital releases.


Releases are distributed internationally via SRD / IDOL.


Faith and Industry remains proudly independent.
