About Faith and Industry

Faith and Industry is a London-based independent record label founded and run by musician and producer Capitol K (Kristian Craig Robinson).

Around the year 2000, I was enjoying the spotlight that my work as Capitol K had brought me. Curious about the artists I was meeting, I began hosting shows and events around East London. At that time, my label briefly released music from both Capitol K and Patrick Wolf before going on a long hiatus.

2018, I'd become very active at TRC (Total Refreshment Centre), where I built up a recording studio and once again connected with artists who sat outside the traditional music industry. This led me to revive Faith And Industry, which began releasing debut material for a range of artists and groups.

By 2020, I had planned an ambitious schedule of releases. When the world went into lockdown, I stubbornly pushed forward and kept the label active throughout that period.

Today, we are on our 37th release and continuing to serve as an outlet for the music and artists I encounter.


Releases are distributed internationally via SRD / IDOL.


Faith and Industry remains proudly independent.
